Policy Privacy

1. Introduction

At our creative agency, protecting your privacy is a responsibility we take seriously. We believe trust is the foundation of every successful relationship, whether with clients, partners, or website visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you interact with our website, services, or communication channels. Our goal is to be transparent about our data practices and ensure you feel confident engaging with us.

When you visit our website, contact us, submit an inquiry, or collaborate with us on a project, certain information may be collected to help us deliver better services and improve your overall experience. This may include personal details you voluntarily provide, as well as technical data automatically collected through your interaction with our digital platforms.

2. Information we collect

  1. Personal information:
    • Full name
    • Email address
    • Phone number
    • Company name
    • Job title
    • Project details or inquiry messages
    • Any information voluntarily submitted through forms
  2. Technical & usage information
    • IP address
    • Browser type and version
    • Device information
    • Operating system
    • Pages visited and time spent
    • Referring URLs
  3. Cookies & tracking technologies
    • Session cookies
    • Performance cookies
    • Analytics cookies
    • Functional cookies

3. How we use your information

Your personal information allows us to respond to inquiries, communicate about projects, and provide the services you request. This includes contacting you regarding proposals, updates, support, or important service-related information. We may also use your details to manage ongoing client relationships and ensure smooth collaboration throughout project timelines.

  • Responding to inquiries and project requests
  • Providing and improving our services
  • Personalizing user experience
  • Improving website functionality and performance
  • Communicating updates, offers, or important notices
  • Internal analytics and performance monitoring
  • Legal, security, and compliance purposes

4. Legal basis for processing data

In many cases, processing is based on your consent, which you provide when submitting forms, contacting us, or agreeing to receive communications. You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time without affecting. We also process data when it is necessary for the performance of a contract or to take steps prior to entering into a contract.

  • Your consent
  • Contractual necessity
  • Legal obligations
  • Legitimate business interests

5. How we share your information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Information may be shared only with:

  • Trusted service providers (hosting, analytics, email services)
  • Internal team members involved in your project
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Business partners under strict confidentiality agreements

*All third parties are required to handle data securely and responsibly.

6. Data security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or loss. Our approach to data security is designed to align with industry standards and best practices while supporting the safe and reliable operation of our services.

  • Secure servers and encrypted connections
  • Restricted access to personal data
  • Regular security reviews and updates
  • Secure data storage practices

*While we take strong precautions, no online system is completely risk-free.

7. Data retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce agreements

*Once data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

8. Third-Party links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, tools, or services that are provided for your convenience or informational purposes. These external sites operate independently from us and are governed by their own privacy policies and practices. We do not control and are not responsible for the content, security, or data handling practices of any third-party websites.

When you click on a third-party link and leave our website, any information you provide is subject to the privacy policies of that external site. We strongly encourage you to review the privacy policies and terms of use.
